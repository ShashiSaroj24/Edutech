A Few Words About The EduTech

The EduTech project is designed to revolutionize education through comprehensive data analysis, statistics, and predictive modeling. It examines critical factors influencing student success, course effectiveness, and dropout rates. It includes statistical insights, course evaluations, and predictive modeling to assess factors affecting dropout likelihood.

EduTech enhances personalized learning experiences, ensures student retention, and improves overall educational outcomes, creating a data-driven ecosystem for student success and institutional growth, improve educational outcomes, supporting personalized learning paths and effective resource allocation.

Analytics

Education Analytics examines critical metrics to understand and improve educational outcomes. Key areas include education expenditure as a percentage of GDP, literacy rates, primary and secondary school enrollment rates, and the balance between literacy and illiteracy rates.
